Measuring Carbon Emissions in Web Development

Welcome to our research project exploring the environmental impact of popular web development frameworks. In this study, we aim to evaluate and compare the carbon emissions generated by websites built using different frameworks such as JavaScript, Angular, React, and Vue. As the digital landscape evolves, understanding the ecological footprint of web development becomes crucial. Join us in uncovering insights that contribute to more sustainable practices in the world of technology.
